5 Stoic Tips

We already know more about Stoicism and that the point of this mindset is not to suffer, or to look for suffering. Actually it is about finding true joy despite the situation be it positive or negative. The key is not the situation, but how we respond to the situation. Here are some tips that will help us to embrace this in a better way.

Best Stoic Quotes

One of the most interesting parts of Seneca's letters is that he's writing to his friend Lucillius and they clearly have this exchange going where they give each other a quote. Then Seneca goes “Oh! I forgot to give you your quote today” or “before I wrap up let me give you your quote for the day”. He addresses this at one point and he says “One way to get to wisdom is to find a quote, an idea, something that gets you better each day”

Keys To Happiness

Happiness isn't having everything we want. It's not excitement all the time. It's not getting everything you want. I think it's a deeper place inside of us. It's when we're realizing the potential that we have as a human beings in all your forms and aspects of our lives.
