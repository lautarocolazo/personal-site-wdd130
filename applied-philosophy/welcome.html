Why Philosophy?

We find ourselves in a moment where every day we are bombarded with news, and false information, where they compare us, where they do everything possible so that we buy and consume all kinds of content. This has caused a global mood imbalance, where we only feel stress, anxiety, and depression. But what if the solution was easier than we think? The ancient philosophers dedicated their lives to finding the best way to approach those problems. On this website, we teach you about Stoicism and how we can apply daily ancient philosophy in a simple way and change our vision of the world instead of changing the world so as not to be affected by negative events. “Everyone thinks about changing the world, but no one thinks about changing himself” - Leo Tolstoy.
